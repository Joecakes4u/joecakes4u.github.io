ILY

Posted by Donne Caballero on December 22, 2015

I love you ...
I don't tell you that I love you
To get you to say it back;
I tell you that I love you cause
I'm thoroughly convinced that:
I'll lay my life down for you,
put your needs above my own;
make sure you get your fill
and you feel welcomed in my home.

But know my love ain't perfect:
No it cannot cast out fear;
By its own it will not keep me
loving you when strife is near.
For witho’ut the love that God pours out
I'm quick to say "Goodbye".
For it's His lo’ve that keeps us strong
And we will stand here side by side

That's true to what He calls us for
And know that He provides
the love that pours within and through
the broken jars He set aside.

I love you ain't a ticket

To your wallet or your heart
It's an overflow of love and faith
and hope that cannot live apart
From God, and I know if you
Say you love me
What you also mean to say
Is yo’u love God; it's through Him
That you're loving me this way

Wish I could say I love my mom and dad, my sister Anne
my brothers, and my sisters, the whole family and
All my enemies and frenemies--
I can't do it on my own
But solely through my God:
Foundation, Rock, and Cornerstone

For God so loved the world He
gave his one begotten Son
That whoever believes won't perish
But have eternal life
And to this truth I will abide
The battle has been won
I shall walk knowing that I am loved
And He shall be my light

So I'll speak my truth one more time
To close I'll spit this verse
The only way I say I love you
Is through God who loves us first
